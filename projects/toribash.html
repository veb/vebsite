Toribash

www.toribash.com

A veritable longrunner, Toribash is an odd story in indie gaming, as it is still being played by thousands of players since its inception in early 2006. Think of it as playing out kung-fu action scenes, with added strategy as it is also multiplayer and turn-based. And you can customize nearly EVERYTHING about your character

The members of veb have been helpful in growing Toribash. Mike has worn many hats: server administrator, virtual economist, web developer, community manager. If it had anything to do with the game running, Mike has done them all.

Arvin has made many hats... yes. Toribash is also a 3D hat simulator in its latest incarnation. If it had to do with site and ingame graphics Arvin has done those.
