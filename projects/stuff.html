Closed Captioning

website: pleasecaption.me

It started as a test to see how quickly we can caption a video of an (oddly enough) news story about the hearing impaired. It took us from scratch, about an hour's work to make a fully captioned video page using video.js

It is a demonstration that live video can be easily captioned cheaply, quickly.

The site is also a small showcase in developing themes and plugins for the Ghost blogging platform
