Modern Sherlock

website: facebook.com/ModernSherlock

A Facebook page dedicated to the Victorian sleuth and part time Iron Man...

It started off as a small fan page to the critically acclaimed BBC series Sherlock. From nothing, it grew into a 300 thousand strong community of fellow fans complete with active discussions, storytelling... everything you want from a community of enthusiasts. Which must have caught the eye of the BBC, for they cloned the likes from Modern Sherlock to their official facebook page and subsequently banned our page. A riveting firsthand account can be read here.

Undettered (and with apologies from Facebook themselves), the community was reinstated and continued to grow. We've moved on, but the page is still very much active
